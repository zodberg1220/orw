interesting fact about camel
  1. Camels are social animals who roam the deserts in search of food and water with up to 30 other individuals
  2. With the exception of rutting males competing for females, camels are very peaceful animals who rarely exhibit aggression
  3. Contrary to popular misconception, camels do not store water in their humps



interesting facts about chameleons
  1. The word chameleon means ground lion
  2. Chameleons evolved 65 million years ago
  3. Chameleons prefer life in the forest and they adapted to the arboreal locomotion
